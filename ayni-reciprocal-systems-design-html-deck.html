Style Statement / V2

Reciprocal Systems Design

Ayni's visual style is reciprocal systems design: structured, warm, and quietly powerful. It takes the language of organizational health - trust, capacity, leadership, communication, and resilience - and frames it through reciprocity rather than extraction.

The aesthetic should feel grounded in mutual care, but still rigorous enough for leaders navigating complexity. Ayni is not about fixing people inside broken systems; it is about rebuilding the conditions where people and organizations can thrive together.
